⭐ Bonus Questions

Each round will have a closest-wins bonus question.

🥇
5 pts
Closest guess. If two or more players tie, all receive 5 pts.
🥈
2 pts
Second closest — only awarded if there is no tie for first. If two or more players tie for second, all receive 2 pts.
🃏 Jokers

The player or players who finish last after a round ends will be given a joker. These will vary in scope but will all have the aim of allowing the player to regain points on the others.
